Our Story .

We are architects inspired by both rich city life and beautiful nature and landscape in New York, California, and Seattle. We are licensed architects with more than eight years experiences in all types of architecture, including residential, commercial, offices, retail, transportation, urban design and etc. We work on projects in all scales, including furniture, furnishing, house, apartments, architecture, landscape, urban design, and etc. We work on projects in all phases, including concept design, schematic design, and construction. We help client streamline design and construction process with experiences in DOB filing and construction administration.

We are here to explore life, built environment, and nature with every valued client. We listen, communicate, and collaborate with clients to reach their goals and build their dreams. We are missioned to deliver projects with unique experiences, high quality, and lots of fulfillment.
